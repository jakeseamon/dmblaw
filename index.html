DAVID M. BACKENSTOE, ESQUIRE

DAVID M. BACKENSTOE, ESQUIRE

General Litigation

Attorney David Backenstoe's practice is located in Hellertown, where he specializes in the following areas of the law: Municipal Law, Land _, Zoning, Family Law, Personal Injury and Wills and Estates. David currently serves as Solicitor to five (5) municipal entities throughout the Lehigh Valley area. David is admitted to practice in all Pennsylvania courts, the Federal District Court and the 3rd Circuit Court of Appeals.

David Backenstoe



Born in Allentown, PA, Attorney Backenstoe is deeply rooted in the Lehigh Valley as he continues to live and work here.
David graduated from Moravian Academy in 1980. As a graduate from Pennsylvania State University, he received his Backelor of Arts in Political Science in 1984. He then received his Juris Doctorate from The Dickinson School of Law in 1987.
David and his wife, Kelly, live in Center Valley and have four (4) children: Andrew, age 22, Abbie, age 21, Madison, age 19 and Olivia, age 17.
